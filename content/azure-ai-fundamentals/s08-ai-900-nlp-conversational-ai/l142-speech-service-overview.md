## 中文短总结

Azure Speech Service 三大能力：1) **Speech-to-Text**（STT）：将语音转为文字（实时转录/批处理音频文件）；2) **Text-to-Speech**（TTS）：将文字转为自然语音（Neural voices，可控制 pitch/rate/volume/pronunciation）；3) **Speech Translation**：跨语言语音翻译。使用 SSML（Speech Synthesis Markup Language）微调语音输出。

## 考试要点

- Speech Service 三大能力：STT / TTS / Speech Translation
- STT：实时转录 or 批量处理
- TTS：Neural voices + SSML 控制
- SSML = Speech Synthesis Markup Language
- Speech Translation = 跨语言语音翻译

## English Short Summary

Azure Speech Service has three main capabilities: 1) Speech-to-Text (real-time transcription or batch audio processing); 2) Text-to-Speech (natural neural voices, control pitch/rate/volume via SSML); 3) Speech Translation (cross-language). SSML = Speech Synthesis Markup Language for fine-tuning voice output.
