## 中文短总结

STT 实操：Foundry → Playgrounds → Audio Playground → Speech → Real Time Transcription。可上传音频文件或使用麦克风实时录制 → 右侧显示文本转录结果。应用：视频字幕生成（提取音频→转录文字）、会议记录、客户电话转录。这是 Playground 演示，实际使用通过程序调用 Speech SDK。

## English Short Summary

STT hands-on: Foundry → Audio Playground → Speech → Real Time Transcription. Upload audio file or record via microphone → text transcript appears on right side. Applications: video captioning (extract audio → transcribe text), meeting notes, customer call transcription. Playground demo; actual use via Speech SDK in programs.
