## 中文短总结

Key Phrase Extraction：从文本中自动提取最重要的词/短语。在 Foundry → Language Playground → Extract Key Phrases 中测试。示例："The hotel had an amazing rooftop pool, but the check-in process was very slow" → 提取：amazing rooftop pool, hotel, process, room。应用：每天数百条客户评论 → 自动提取高频关键短语（如 "excellent customer support", "slow delivery"）。

## 考试要点

- Key Phrase Extraction = 提取文本中最重要的词/短语
- 捕获文本核心含义
- 应用：客户评论分析、文档摘要
- 在 Language Playground 中测试

## English Short Summary

Key Phrase Extraction: automatically pulls out most important words/phrases from text. In Foundry → Language Playground → Extract Key Phrases. Example: "The hotel had an amazing rooftop pool..." → extracts: amazing rooftop pool, hotel, process, room. Application: hundreds of daily customer reviews → auto-extract frequent key phrases.
