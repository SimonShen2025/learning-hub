## 中文短总结

Azure Document Intelligence Service：自动从 PDF/图像/表单中提取文本、键值对、表格和文档结构。使用 AI 模型 + 高级 OCR。预构建模型：Invoice（提取客户/供应商信息）、Receipt（费用管理）。超越简单 OCR：理解文档结构（如自动识别 vendor name, invoice ID, date, line items）。也可训练自定义模型。在 Foundry → AI Services → Documents 中测试。

## 考试要点

- Document Intelligence = 从文档提取结构化数据
- 超越 OCR：理解文档语义结构
- 预构建模型：Invoice, Receipt, ID Document, Business Card
- 提取：text + key-value pairs + tables + structure
- 可训练自定义模型

## English Short Summary

Azure Document Intelligence: automatically extract text, key-value pairs, tables, and structure from PDFs/images/forms using AI models + advanced OCR. Pre-built models: Invoice (vendor/customer details), Receipt (expense management). Beyond simple OCR: understands document structure (auto-identifies vendor name, invoice ID, date, line items). Custom models also available.
