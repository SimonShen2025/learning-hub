## 中文短总结

公平性（Fairness）：AI 系统必须平等对待所有人，不因种族、性别、年龄、残疾等歧视。AI 模型在历史数据上训练，但历史数据可能反映社会偏见→如不检测和纠正，AI 会放大不公平。示例：招聘 AI 偏向某性别/种族；信用评分模型对某群体不公。要求：多样化代表性数据训练、跨人口统计测试偏差。公平 AI = 公正、无偏见、无歧视。

## 考试要点

- Fairness = AI 平等对待所有人，无歧视
- 历史数据中的偏见会被 AI 学习和放大
- 需要：多样化数据、跨群体偏差测试
- 示例：招聘偏见、信用评分不公
- Microsoft 六大原则的最后一个
- **六大原则汇总**：Accountability, Reliability & Safety, Privacy & Security, Transparency, Inclusiveness, Fairness

## English Short Summary

Fairness: AI must treat all people equally without discrimination based on race, gender, age, disability, etc. Historical training data may reflect societal biases — if not detected/corrected, AI amplifies unfairness. Examples: biased hiring AI, unfair credit scoring. Requirements: diverse representative training data, bias testing across demographics. All 6 principles: Accountability, Reliability & Safety, Privacy & Security, Transparency, Inclusiveness, Fairness.
