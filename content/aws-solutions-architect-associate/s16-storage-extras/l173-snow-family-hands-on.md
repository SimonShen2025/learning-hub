## 中文短总结

本讲演示 Snow Family 设备订购流程。选择 Job 类型：Import to S3（AWS 寄设备给你）、Export from S3（从 S3 导出到设备）、Local Compute（仅边缘计算）。选择设备型号（Storage Optimized 210TB 或 Compute Optimized）。配置定价选项、目标 S3 Bucket、加密方式、IAM Service Role。设置收货地址和运输速度（一日达/两日达），配置通知。提交后等待接收物理 Snowball 设备。

## English Short Summary

Hands-on Snow Family ordering process. Choose job type: Import to S3, Export from S3, or Local Compute. Select device (Storage Optimized 210TB or Compute Optimized). Configure pricing, target S3 bucket, encryption, IAM service role. Set shipping address and speed (1-day or 2-day). After submission, receive the physical Snowball device, load data, and ship it back to AWS.
