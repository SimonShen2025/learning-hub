## 中文短总结

实操演示 CloudWatch Logs Live Tail 功能：创建 Log Group 和 Log Stream，点击"Start Tailing"实时查看新日志事件。当日志被发布到 CloudWatch Logs 时，Live Tail 界面实时显示，适合调试场景。注意：每天约 1 小时免费使用，使用后记得关闭 Live Tail 会话避免产生费用。

## English Short Summary

Hands-on demo of CloudWatch Logs Live Tail: create a Log Group and Log Stream, click "Start Tailing" to see log events appear in real-time as they're posted. Useful for debugging. Note: approximately 1 hour/day of free usage — close the Live Tail session afterward to avoid charges.
