## 中文短总结

本讲演示了在 Route 53 中注册域名的流程。包括选择域名、设置 Duration 和 Auto Renew、填写联系信息、启用 Privacy Protection 防止垃圾邮件。注册完成后，Route 53 自动创建 Hosted Zone，包含 NS Record（指向 AWS DNS 服务器）和 SOA Record。域名注册费用最低 $12/年，注册可能需要几分钟到几小时。

## English Short Summary

This hands-on demonstrates registering a domain in Route 53, covering domain name selection, duration/auto-renew settings, contact information, and privacy protection. After registration, Route 53 automatically creates a Hosted Zone with NS and SOA records. Domain registration costs minimum $12/year and may take minutes to hours to complete.
