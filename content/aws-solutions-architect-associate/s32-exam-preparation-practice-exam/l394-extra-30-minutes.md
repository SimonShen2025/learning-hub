## 中文短总结

非英语母语者额外 30 分钟：在认证门户 → Exam Accommodations → 申请 **ESL +30 Minutes**（English as Second Language）。可上传证明文件。批准后**永不过期**，适用于所有未来考试。**注意**：如果之前已经预约了考试，需要先取消，批准后再重新预约才能应用额外时间。

## English Short Summary

Extra 30 minutes for non-native English speakers: Certification portal → Exam Accommodations → request **ESL +30 Minutes**. Upload documents if needed. Once approved, it **never expires** and applies to all future exams. **Note**: if you already have a scheduled exam, cancel it first, then reschedule after approval to include the extra time.
